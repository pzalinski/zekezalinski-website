Where I've landed along the way.

Director of User Experience & Design June 2021 – May 2026

Recruited directly by the CTO — a relationship built over five years at Maestro Health — to assess and restructure a fragmented UX organization across a portfolio of 8–10 acquired SaaS products, before being elevated to lead it. The work was equal parts organizational design and creative leadership: building the systems, standards, and cross-functional partnerships that let a lean team of five run a well-organized, high-output design operation across a complex multi-product environment.

  • Authored the organization's first UX Design Guide, establishing the process discipline, documentation standards, and scope clarity the team needed to operate consistently and grow.
  • Designed and directed the Accelerate Design System (ADS) in Figma, then partnered with Front End engineering to scale it across 6 simultaneous Scrum teams as the production backbone of all product development.
  • Directed two full brand overhauls across the product suite, unifying the visual experience of a portfolio that had reflected the scattered identities of a dozen acquired companies.
  • Championed early AI adoption, including Figma AI and Lovable, partnering with Product to accelerate ideation and bring design-system-aligned prototypes to stakeholder conversations faster.
Director of Experience Design 2015 – 2020

Health insurance company aiming to make healthcare people-friendly. Crafted user experiences across 8+ internal and public tools; partnered with business stakeholders, analysts, and the full tech department to deliver simple UI over complex data systems.

Primary Experience Design Lead 2013 – 2015

At Rocket Web: UX Lead and primary designer for UX/visual deliverables, bridging client business goals with design execution; also helped brand Rocket Web's own market offerings. At 50,000feet: supported digital projects and client engagements, worked alongside the UX Director on Abbott Labs projects, supported the launch of 50,000feet's new site.

Design Director 2012 – 2013

First remote office with a director across all four pillars (sales, development, engagement, design). Led client discovery, stakeholder interviews, alignment sessions; managed how content strategy, experience architecture, and design communicated with dev/QA/PM; contributed to concept design for Grant Thornton, American College of Surgeons, DeVry University.

Associate Creative Director 2011 – 2012

Oversaw internal creative process; consulted on company-wide project management and communication; client-facing rep from discovery through handoff; oversaw the LCG brand through a new agency site launch and marketing efforts; represented LCG at IRWD 2011.

Senior Design Lead 2010 – 2011

Agency named 2010 Magento Partner of the Year. Drove projects end-to-end (discovery → concepts → art direction); supervised design integration/production/QA in development; represented Gorilla at the 2011 Internet Retailer Web Design Conference.
