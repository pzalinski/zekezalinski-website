We are the experiences we have and create.

I'm an experience person. I draw so much inspiration and happiness from the experiences that I have, and in the ones that I help create on the job. I think that every trip taken, every dinner had with friends, every group of friends or coworkers and of course every project at work is a collection of experiences that a positive attitude and inspired ideas can influence.

I dare say that we all want our life to be smooth no matter at home or work. I put in the time in my personal life to achieve a balance with family and friends, and I bring that effort to the table when I partner with a company or join a team. I enjoy people that can take the time to ask themselves, "What are we solving for?" I feel if we can pause from time to time and ask that question, great work can be done.

Enjoying life experiences as an artist, designer, creative, husband, father, son and friend.
